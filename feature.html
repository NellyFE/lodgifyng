About Us

LodgifyNG is a Platform For Transforming the Real Estate and Housing Industry

Our mission is to create a seamless, comprehensive platform that empowers users to access all real estate and housing services at the tap of a finger. With a deep understanding of the local market and a commitment to innovation, we aim to transform how Nigerians buy, rent, invest in, and manage properties.

Key Features of LodgifyNG

Property Transactions: Users can Buy, sell, rent, or let lands, apartments, offices, and shops all through our streamlined platform.

Hotel & Rental Booking: Users can Book hotels, motels, apartments, event halls, and other spaces with ease.

Find-A-Roomie: Users can Match with living partners (roommates) based on shared interests and preferences, making shared living easier and more enjoyable.

Shared Living: Unlock additional shared living features by gaining a roomie badge after successful roomie matching. These features enhance the shared living experience with activities, rewards and tools to help ease the challenges of cohabitation.

LodgiMove: Users can Get access to reliable moving services and logistics for hassle-free relocations, with options for delivery of all purchases made on our platform.

Hotel & Rental Booking: LodgiFinance (LodgiFi): i Real Estate Crowdfunding (L-REC): Users can Participate in or start crowdfunding projects to develop real estate initiatives.

ii. Real Estate Investment Trust (L-REIT): Users can Invest in real estate by purchasing shares in properties owned by LodgifyNG, making property ownership more accessible.

Own2Let & Own2Use: Users can Opt for properties with the flexibility to buy for personal use or as rental investments, where we can offer our professional property management services.

Pimp My Space (PMS): Users can Access top-tier interior design, decoration, construction and renovation services for homes, offices, and more.

LodgiMart: Users can Access a marketplace for housing-related goods, where users can buy, sell, and even become vendors for items like furniture, decor, and appliances.

Agent Portal: We provide real estate agents with their own back-office tools to manage and update their property listings seamlessly and simplify house rent collecting

Landlord Portal: We empower landlords to manage properties and gain financial insights into their portfolios.

LodgifyNG Academy: Users can Access educational resources and expert consultation on real estate investment.

Lodgicurrency: Our in-app currency system, allowing users to transact easily using LodgiCash, with the potential to earn rewards through LodgiCoin for referrals, engagement, and completing tasks.

Workshop: Users can Find and hire professional housing-related artisans like carpenters, plumbers, electricians, painters, welders, and more for quality, reliable home services.
